ABOUT

FROM THE FARM INTO THE CUP & HOME IN LAGOS

PERFECT COFFEE MOMENTS & WELCOMING ATMOSPHERE

The birth of the Smooth Coffee can be traced back to 1999 when the first Coffeeshop opened its doors to its guests on a historical September day in Vienna.

Characterized by a very special and unimitable atmosphere, the Smooth Coffee is a new, unique interpretation of the ever-popular traditional Viennese coffee house culuture, which was included in the UNESCO list of ''intangible cultural heritage'' in 2011.
